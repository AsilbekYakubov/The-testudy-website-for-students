The Present Simple Tense

The Present Simple da 3-shaxs birlikda (he, she, it) fe’lning asos
shakliga s, es yoki ies qo‘shiladi.



  • Oddiy fe'llarga shunchaki s qo'shing - Masalan: travel >travels,
    give > gives, play >plays
  • s, ss, sh, ch, x, va o harflari bilan tugaydigan fe’llarga es
    qo‘shing - Masalan: wash > washes, mix > mixes, go >goes
  • Fe'llarning undoshdan keyin y harfi bilan tugashi uchun
    (unli bo'lmagan har qanday harf) y ni i ga o'zgartiring va es qo'shing.
    Masalan: study > studies, fly > flies

Quyidagi fikrlarni ifodalash uchun hozirgi sodda zamondan
foydalanamiz:



  • Faktlarni yoki umumiy haqiqatlarni bayon qilish
  • Odatlar yoki urf-odatlarni ifodalash uchun
  • Kelajakdagi rejalar uchun (ko'pincha dasturlar va jadvallar haqida)
  • Hazillar va hikoyalar aytib berish yoki real vaqtda sport voqealari
    haqida xabar berish.

Misol:


  • The sun sets in the west.
  • We produce lasers for cosmetic surgery.
  • They move into their new home next week.
  • So, I go to Mr. D and say “I deserve a better mark in this class”.
  • Jones stops in mid-court and passes the ball to Schuster.


Present Simple ni shakllantirish



Subject Verb Rest of sentence
I / You / We / They sleep late on Saturdays
He / She / It goes to the beach every weekend



Present Simple dagi vaqt ifodalari



Present simple dagi eng keng tarqalgan vaqt iboralari: usually, always, never, on Wednesdays, every Wednesday, twice a week, once a month, in general, every other day.

Time expressions made up of one word are placed between the subject and the verb in positive sentences and questions and between the auxiliary verb and main verb in negative sentences.



  • I always study hard for exams.
  • Do you usually speak to him like that?

    Ikki yoki undan ortiq so'zdan tuzilgan vaqt iboralari gap boshida yoki
    oxirida va odatda savollarning oxirida joylashtiriladi.
  • Ben goes to football practice every Tuesday.
  • In general, I believe that all people can live in peace.
  • you go to the supermarket every week?


Negative Sentences in the Present
Simple Tense



3-shaxs (he, she, it) inkorini qisqartirganda, not in o ni olib tashlang
va apostrofni (‘) qo‘shing does not > doesn’t.



Inkor gaplar tuzishda odatda don’t va doesn’t yordamchi fe’llaridan +
fe’lning asos shaklini ishlatamiz.


Subject Auxillery verb Verb in base form Rest of sentence
I / You / We / They don’t (do not) eat late at night
He / She / It doesn’t (does not) watch TV every day


Yes/No Questions in the Present Simple


“Ha” yoki “Yo‘q” bilan javob beradigan savol yaratish uchun savolni Do yoki Does bilan boshlang, so‘ngra egani (harakatni bajaruvchi shaxs yoki narsa) keyin fe’lning asosiy shaklini qo‘shing va shundan keyingina qolganini qo‘shing.


Auxiliary Verb Subject Verb in base form Rest of sentence
Do I / you / we / they drive to the city on Mondays
Does he / she / it break down often



  • When do you want to meet me?
  • Why does Beth always complain so much?
  • How much does the ticket cost?
  • Why don’t you ever go on vacation?


Tag Questions in the Present Simple


Tag questions - jumla oxiridagi qisqa savollardir. Ular faqat siz gaplashayotgan odam nimani nazarda tutganingizni tushunganiga ishonch hosil qilish yoki aytganingizni ta'kidlash uchun ishlatiladi.



  • John likes me, doesn’t he?
  • All those girls speak French, don’t they?
  • Keisha doesn’t speak Spanish, does she
  • Those boys don’t play sports, do they?

Exercises for the Present Simple


Misollardagi kabi fe'lning to'g'ri shaklini to'ldiring.

  • Sheila doesn’t do the family accounts. (not do) Her husband does them.
