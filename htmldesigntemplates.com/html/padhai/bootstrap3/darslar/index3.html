The Past Simple Tense


Imlo maslahati

O'tmishdagi oddiy fe'llar

Biz o'tmishda boshlangan va o'tmishda tugagan harakatni tasvirlash uchun past simple so'zidan foydalanamiz. Bu yigirma yil oldin yoki ikki daqiqa oldin sodir bo'lgan voqea bo'lishi mumkin. Boshlandi. To'xtadi. Tugadi.



  • Ko'pgina fe'llarga ed qo'shing. Masalan: talk > talk ed , employ > employed
  • Agar qisqa fe'l undosh-unli-undosh bilan tugasa, oxirgi harfni ikki barobarga oshiring va keyin ed qo'shing. Masalan: stop > stopped, top > topped Biroq, agar fe'l w, x yoki y bilan tugasa, oxirgi harfni ikki marta ko'paytirmang. Masalan: play > play ed, mix > mixed.
  • Uzunroq so‘zlar bilan aytganda, agar fe’lning oxirgi bo‘g‘ini undosh-unli-undosh bilan tugasa va bu bo‘g‘in urg‘uli bo‘lsa, oxirgi undoshni ikki barobar qilib, so‘ng ed qo‘shing. Masalan: prefer > preferred Biroq, agar birinchi bo'g'in urg'ulangan bo'lsa, oxirgi harfni ikki baravar oshirmang. Masalan: enter > entered
  • Agar fe'l e bilan tugasa, shunchaki d qo'shing. Masalan: create > created , live > lived
  • Agar fe'l + y undoshi bilan tugasa, y ni i ga o'zgartiring va ed qo'shing. Masalan: try > tried


O'tgan sodda zamon juda oddiy. Asosiy muammo - bu imlo qoidalari, ularni quyida topasiz.


1. I visited a client in London yesterday.

2. She planned the event all by herself.


Past simple uchun ishlatiladigan eng keng tarqalgan vaqt iboralari: kecha, bir hafta (oy, yil) oldin, oxirgi (oy, yil, hafta oxiri, dushanba) kechasi, kechagi kun, ikki kun (oy, yil) oldin. Vaqt ifodasi gapning boshida yoki oxirida paydo bo'ladi - hech qachon gapning o'rtasida emas.

Forming the Past Simple



Subject Verb + d, ed, ied or irregular form (V2) Rest of Sentence
I / He / She / It / You / We / They walked to the shop yesterday
slept late last Saturday

Past simple odatda fe'lning asosiy shakliga d, ed yoki ied qo'shilishi bilan hosil bo'ladi, ammo ingliz tilida o'tgan zamonda butunlay boshqacha shaklga ega bo'lgan tartibsiz fe'llar ko'p. Ba'zi odamlar buni fe'lning V2 shakli deb atashadi. Eng yaxshisi ularni yodlab olishga harakat qilishdir.



Negative Sentences in the Past Simple Tense

3-shaxs (he, she, it) inkorini qisqartirganda, not in o ni olib tashlang va apostrof (‘) qo‘shing. did not > didn’t

Past simpleda inkor gap yaratish uchun didn’t (did not) + fe’lning asosiy shaklini ishlating.

Eslatma: Urg'u yaratmoqchi bo'lgan vaqt uchun uzun shakllarni (did not) saqlang.
Gapirayotganda "did not" ga urg'u qo'ying.



Subject didn’t + verb in the base form Rest of Sentence

didn’t sleep late last Saturday

2.He didn’t steal those ideas from the company.

Wh-Questions in the Past Simple


Wh- savollar - bu javoblarida ko'proq ma'lumot talab qiladigan savollar. Odatda wh- so'zlari what, where, when, why, who, how, how many, how much.

Wh-savolini yaratish uchun wh-so'zidan boshlang, so'ngra did (yoki inkor savol uchun) qo'shing, so'ngra mavzuni (harakatni bajaradigan shaxs yoki narsa), keyin esa fe'lning asosiy shaklini qo'shing. va shundan keyingina gapning qolgan qismini qo'shing.

You / We / They		 sell the house
Why didn’t rescue me

1. When didyou buythat sweater?

2. Why didthe computer break down?

3. How long didthe train journey take?

4. Why didn’tyou tellme about the accident?



Past Simple-da savollarni belgilang


Teg savollari jumla oxiriga teglangan qisqa savollardir. Ular faqat siz gaplashayotgan odam nimani nazarda tutganingizni tushunganiga ishonch hosil qilish yoki aytganingizni ta'kidlash uchun ishlatiladi.



Ular o‘tgan simdagi musbat gapni qo‘llash va so‘ng did not, olmosh (I, you, we, they, he, she, it) va so‘roq belgisini qo‘shish orqali yasaladi.


1.John scored a goal, didn’t he?

2.Their dogs barked all night,didn’t they?


Salbiy belgidan foydalanganda siz ijobiy teg qo'shishingiz mumkin jumla.

1.Kate didn’t take out the rubbish bin, did she?

2.The girls didn’t copy on the examdid they?

Qoidaga ko'ra: Gap ijobiy bo'lsa, teg salbiy bo'ladi.
Gap salbiy bo'lsa, teg ijobiy bo'ladi.
