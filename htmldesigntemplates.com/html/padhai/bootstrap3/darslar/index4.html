The Past Progressive Tense



O'tgan progressiv zamon ko'plab Ingilis tili ona tili bo'lmaganlar uchun qiyin, chunki ko'p tillarda
ekvivalenti yo'q. O'tgan progressiv o'tmishda ma'lum bir vaqtda bajarilgan harakatni tasvirlaydi.
Undan foydalanish mumkin:

O'tmishda boshlangan va boshqa harakat bilan to'xtatilgan harakatni tasvirlash uchun:




  • He was writing an e-mail when the phone rang.
  • When the phone rang, he was writing an e-mail.
  • While he was writing an e-mail, the phone rang


O'tmishda bir vaqtning o'zida bajarilgan ikkita harakatni tavsiflash uchun:



  • I was preparing dinner while Melanie was working upstairs.
  • While Melanie was working upstairs, I was preparing dinner


Imlo maslahati


Fe'l (hozirgi zamon ishtirokchisi)



  • Ko‘pchilik fe’llarga ing qo‘shish. Masalan: play > playing, cry > crying, bark > bark ing
  • e bilan tugaydigan fe’llar uchun e harfini olib tashlang va ing qo‘shing. Masalan: slide > sliding, ride > riding
  • ie bilan tugaydigan fe'llar uchun ie ni y ga o'zgartiring va ing qo'shing. Masalan: die > dying, tie > ty ing
  • Oxirgi bo‘g‘ini undosh-unli-undosh bilan yozilgan va urg‘uli bo‘lgan fe’l uchun ing qo‘shishdan oldin oxirgi harfni ikki baravar oshiring. Masalan: beg > begging , begin > beginning. Biroq: enter > enter ing (oxirgi bo'g'in urg'u qilinmaydi)


O‘tgan progressiv was yoki were va fe’lning ing (hozirgi zamon) shakli yordamida yasaladi.



Subject A form of be + Verbing Rest of Sentence
I / He / She / It was finishing the exam when the bell rang
You / We / They were paying the bill while I was waiting to be seated


Negatives in the Past Progressive (Continuous)


O'tgan progressiv zamondagi inkor fe'lning was not yoki were not + ing (hozirgi zamon) shakli yordamida yaratilgan.
Eslatma: Umuman olganda, bu kelishiklarni inkorda ishlating: wasn't, weren't. Urg'u yaratmoqchi bo'lgan vaqt uchun uzun shakllarni saqlang.



Subject A form of be + Verbing Rest of Sentence
I / He / She / It wasn’t crying when you came home
You / We / They weren’t hiking there when the earthquake hit


  • I wasn’t sleeping when you came home last night.
  • When Ms. Foster came in, the girls weren’t studying.
  • Sam wasn’t lying when he said he loved you.


Yes/No Questions in the Past Progressive (Continuous)



Ha yoki yo‘q bilan javob beriladigan savolni berish uchun Was yoki Were, (salbiy savol uchun Wasn’t yoki Weren’t) bilan boshlang, so‘ngra mavzuni (harakatni bajarayotgan shaxs yoki narsa) tanlang. fe'lning ing (hozirgi zamon) shakli va keyin savolingizning qolgan qismi qo'ying.



A form of Be subject verbing rest of sentence
Was I walking too fast
Was he / she / it shouting while you were trying to sleep
Were you / we / they waiting for her when the plane landed


  • Was I talking to you?
  • Were you writing the report when the electricity went off?
  • When you came home, was he singing in the shower?
  • Wasn’t Tom sitting in the cafe when you drove past?


Wh-Questions in the Past Progressive (Continuous)



Wh- savollar - bu javoblarida ko'proq ma'lumot talab qiladigan savollar. Odatda wh- so'zlari: what, where, when, which, why, who, how. Wh-savolini yaratish uchun Wh-soʻzidan boshlang, keyin was yoki were, soʻng subʼyekt (harakatni bajaradigan shaxs yoki narsa), keyin ing qo'shing, fe'lning (bo'lak) shakli va shundan keyingina gapning qolgan qismini qo'shing.



Wh Word A form of Be subject verbing rest of sentence
Who was I talking to
What was he / she / it doing
When were you / we / they planning on telling me the bad news


  • Where were you standing when the trouble started?
  • Who was I talking to? I can’t remember.
  • Why wasn’t she waiting at the meeting point?
