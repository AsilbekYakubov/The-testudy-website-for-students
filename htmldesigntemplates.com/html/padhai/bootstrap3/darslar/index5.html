The Future Tense



Kelajakni ingliz tilida bir necha usul bilan ifodalash mumkin. Bu erda turli xil imkoniyatlar mavjud:



  • Future Simple: will + base form of the verb
  • Be Going To: am, is, are + going to + base form of the verb
  • Shall: Shall + subject + base form of the verb?
  • Future Progressive: will be + verbing
  • Present Simple and Present Progressive with Future Meaning

Future Progressive (Continuous) – will + be + verbing


Spelling Tip


Verbing (Present Participle)


  • Add ing to most verbs. Ex. play > playing, cry > cry ing, bark > barking
  • For verbs that end in e, remove the e and add ing. Ex: slide > slidiing, ride > riding
  • For verbs that end in ie, change the ie to y and add ing. Ex: die > dying, tie > tying
  • For a verb whose last syllable is written with a consonant-vowel-consonant and is stressed, double the last letter before adding ing. Ex: beg > begg ing, begin > beginning. However: enter > entering (last syllable is not stressed)

    Present Simple and Present Progressive with Future Meaning



    Hozirgi sodda zamon yaqin kelajakdagi rejalar haqida gapirish uchun kelajakni ifodalash uchun ishlatilishi mumkin.


    • Her bus leaves at 6:00 Tuesday morning.

    Hozirgi progressiv zamon kelajakni ifodalash uchun ishlatilishi mumkin, ayniqsa siz oldindan tuzilgan rejalar haqida gapirmoqchi bo'lsangiz. Uni harakat fe'llari bilan ishlatish odatiy holdir.


    • I’m turning in my History assignment tomorrow morning.

    Tag Questions in the Future Tense


    Teg savollari jumla oxiriga teglangan qisqa savollardir. Ular faqat siz gaplashayotgan odam nimani nazarda tutganingizni tushunganiga ishonch hosil qilish yoki aytganingizni ta'kidlash uchun ishlatiladi.

    Ular kelasi zamon shakllarida ijobiy gapni qo‘llash va tegishli ko‘makchi (yordamchi) va olmosh (I, Your, we, they, he, she, it) va so‘roq belgisini qo‘shish orqali yasaladi.


    • John will call her tomorrow, won’t he?
    • All those girls will be sitting quietly, won’t they?
    • You’re coming over tonight, aren’t you?
    • That bee is going to sting me, isn’t it?

    Salbiy jumladan foydalanganda ijobiy teg qo'shishingiz mumkin.


    • Carey won’t report me, will she?
    • The kids aren’t going to play soccer tomorrow, are they?
    • You’re not working tomorrow, are you?

    Qoidaga ko'ra: Gap ijobiy bo'lsa, teg salbiy bo'ladi. Gap salbiy bo'lsa, teg ijobiy bo'ladi.
