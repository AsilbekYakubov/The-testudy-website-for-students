The Past Perfect Simple Tense


Foydali maslahat


Past Perfect Simpledagi vaqt ifodalari


Allaqachon, for, since va yet vaqt iboralari hozirgi mukammal simpleda bo‘lgani kabi past perfect simpleda ham ishlatilishi mumkin. Boshqa vaqt ifodalarini ishlatish uchun quyidagi qoidalarni unutmang:



  • Use after, as soon as, the moment that, until before using the past perfect simple.
    Ex: After she had moved out, I found her notes./ I didn’t say anything until she had finished talking.
  • Use before, when, by the time before the past simple:
    Ex. Before I knew it, she had run out the door. / By the time he phoned her, she had found someone new.

Past perfect simple o'tmishdagi boshqa harakatdan oldin sodir bo'lgan bir harakatni tasvirlash uchun ishlatiladi.


Ko'p hollarda to'liq jumla ikki xil zamon bilan ikki qismga yoziladi:


  • The past perfect simple, to refer to the action that happened first or earlier
  • The past simple to refer to the action that happened second or later

Ba'zan past perfect simple o'z-o'zidan qo'llaniladi va undan keyin sodir bo'lgan ish-harakat tushuniladi.


  • After Sofie had finished her work, she went to lunch. (First she finished her work and then she went to eat lunch.)
  • I washed the floor when the painter had gone. (First the painter left and then I washed the floor.)
  • Harold had known about it for a while. (First he knew about it, then others knew about it)

Past perfect sodda zamon V3 (o‘tgan zamon) bilan birga had yordamchi fe’li yordamida yasaladi. Muntazam fe'lning V3 (o'tgan zamon) shakli xuddi o'tmishdagi oddiy fe'lga o'xshaydi:


  • walk > walked / study > studied / stop > stopped / create > created

Ingliz tilida bir nechta tartibsiz fe'llar mavjud. Ularni yodlab olish foydalidir.

  • By the time Doris got to the party, everyone had gone home.

Contractions in the Past Perfect Simple



Punctuation Tip



When you begin a sentence with a time expression, put a comma (,) after the first part of the sentence.
We often contract the subject (the person or thing that had done the action) and had:



  • I had > I’d – After I’d used the phone, I paid the bill.
  • He had > He’d / She has > She’d / It has > It’d – It’d happened so quickly, I didn’t notice.
  • We had > We’d / You have > You’d /They are > They’d – We’d just gotten home, when we heard the blast outside.

Wh-Questions in the Past Perfect Simple


Wh- savollar - bu javoblarida ko'proq ma'lumot talab qiladigan savollar. Odatda wh- so'zlari nima, qaerda, qachon, nima uchun, kim, qanday, qancha, qancha.

Wh-savolini yaratish uchun wh-so'zidan boshlang, keyin had, so'ngra sub'ektni (harakatni bajargan shaxs yoki narsa), so'ngra fe'lning V3 (o'tgan ishtirokchi) shaklini qo'shing va shundan keyingina qo'shing. jumlaning qolgan qismi.



  • What had they said that made him so angry?
  • Why had he agreed to work for that salary?
  • How much had he drunk before you got to him?


Tag Questions in the Past Perfect Simple


Teg savollari jumla oxiriga teglangan qisqa savollardir. Ular faqat siz gaplashayotgan odam nimani nazarda tutganingizni tushunganiga ishonch hosil qilish yoki aytganingizni ta'kidlash uchun ishlatiladi.

Ular o‘tgan zamonda oddiy gapni yozish orqali, keyin hadn’t va olmosh (men, you, we, they, he, she, it) va so‘roq belgisini qo‘shish orqali yasaladi.



  • John had known about the cancer for a couple of years, hadn’t he?
  • They had been in business together, hadn’t they?


Salbiy jumladan foydalanganda ijobiy teg qo'shishingiz mumkin.


  • Jennifer hadn’t spoken to you about it, had she?
  • They had never eaten a proper Indian meal, had they?

Qoidaga ko'ra: Gap ijobiy bo'lsa, teg salbiy bo'ladi.


When the sentence is negative, the tag is positive.
