The Present Perfect Simple Verb Tense



The Perfect Tenses




Ko'pgina mahalliy va ingilis tili ona tili bo'lmagan so'zlashuvchilarning fikriga ko'ra, zamonni o'rganish qiyin tuyuladi. Ular ko'pchilik uchun bosh og'rig'iga sabab bo'ladi. Ushbu sahifalarda biz mukammal zamonlarni tushunishni osonlashtiradigan qisqa bo'limlarga ajratamiz.



Foydali maslahat Present Perfectdagi vaqt ifodalari



  • Ma'lum bir yil yoki o'tmishdagi davr bilan > since 2002 / since I was a child
  • Bir necha yil > for twenty years
  • Savollar va negativlar uchun ever va yet dan foydalaning > Have you ever / hasn’t been yet
  • Use before, since, for, already, many times, so far, yet dan gap yoki savollar oxirida foydalaning >



O'tmishda sodir bo'lgan, ammo hozirgi kunga tegishli bo'lgan harakatlarni tasvirlab berilishi:


  • Rani has broken her arm. (U o'tmishda uni sindirgan va hozir qo'lidan foydalana olmaydi.)


O'tmishda boshlangan, lekin hali ham muntazam yoki odatiy asosda sodir bo'layotgan harakatni tasvirlab berilishi:


  • The girls have played tennis at the club since 2005. (Ular 2005-yilda tennis o‘ynashni boshlaganlar va hozir ham o‘sha yerda o‘ynashmoqda. Bu ular hozirda tennis o‘ynayapti degani emas.)


O'tmishda bir necha marta takrorlangan harakatlarni tasvirlab berilishi:


  • I‘ve already made several calls. (Hozirgacha)


The Typical Present Perfect Sentence



Present perfect simpleda tipik jumla hosil qilish uchun mavzuni tanlang ((harakatni bajargan shaxs yoki narsa), yordamchi fe'l qo'shing: has yoki have + the V3 (o'tgan zamon) shakli fe'l va keyin gapning qolgan qismini qo'shing.


Subject have/has +Verb(V3) (Past Participle) Rest of Sentence
I / You / We / They have met him before
He / She / It has lived here for three years


Muntazam fe'lning V3 (o'tgan zamon) shakli xuddi o'tmishdagi oddiy fe'lga o'xshaydi:


  • walk > walked / study > studied / stop > stop ped / create > created


Present Perfect Simpledagi qisqarishlar



Umuman olganda, biz subyekt (harakatni bajargan shaxs yoki narsa) va ega shakli bilan shartnoma tuzamiz:


  • I have > I’ve – I’ve used those images before.
  • He has > He’s / She has > She’s / It has > It’s – He’s already had the surgery.
  • We have > We’ve / You have > You’ve / They are > They’ve – We’ve just gotten home.


3-shaxs birlik (he, she, it) qisqarishlari hozirgi progressivdagi kabi kelishini payqagan bo‘lsangiz kerak. Siz ularni V3 dan foydalanish va jumla kontekstidan ajratishingiz mumkin: he is > He’s eating now. / u (o'g'il bolaga nisbatan)> U allaqachon kechki ovqatni yedi



Urg'u yaratmoqchi bo'lgan vaqt uchun uzun shakllarni saqlang.


  • He has sung that song. I know he has.


Negative Sentences in the Present Perfect Simple Tense



Imlo maslahati


Have/has va inkor fe’lini qisqartirganda, not ni olib tashlang va apostrof qo‘shing (‘)

has not > hasn’t
have not > haven’t

Inkor jumlalarni yaratishda biz odatda hasn't yoki hasn't birga + fe'lning V3 (o'tgan zamon) shaklidan foydalanamiz. Urg'u yaratmoqchi bo'lgan vaqt uchun uzun shakllarni saqlang.



Subject Auxillery Verb Verb in V3 (Past Participle) Rest of Sentence
I / You / We / They haven’t (have not) ridden a bike in many years
He / She / It hasn’t (has not) lost enough weight yet

  • I haven’t eaten at that restaurant in a long time.
  • Jim hasn’t worked on Fridays since he joined the company.
  • My friends haven’t ever gone to France.
  • I have not forgiven you!



Yes/No Questions in the Present Perfect Simple


“Ha” yoki “Yo‘q” bilan javob beradigan savol yaratish uchun jadvalga qarang:



Auxillery Verb Subject Verb in V3 (Past Participle) Rest of Sentence
Have I / you / we / they begun the meeting yet
Has he / she / it answered your letter
Hasn’t he / she / it eaten dinner yet

  • Have you ever gone ice skating?
  • Has Jerry presented his ideas to the CEO yet?


Wh-Questions in the Present Perfect Simple


Wh- savollar - bu javoblarida ko'proq ma'lumot talab qiladigan savollar. Odatda wh- so'zlari:
what, where, when, why, who, how, how many, how much.



Wh-Word Auxiliary Verb Subject Verb in V3 (Past Participle) Rest of Sentence
What have I / you / we / they read lately
Why has he / she / it changed color


  • When have I ever lied to you?
  • Why has Tanya left the country?
  • How much money have you spent sofar?
