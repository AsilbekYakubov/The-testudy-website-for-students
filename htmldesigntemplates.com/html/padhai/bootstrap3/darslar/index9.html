The Past Perfect Progressive Tense



Imlo maslahati



  • Ko‘pchilik fe’llarga ing qo‘shish. Masalan: play > playing, cry > crying, bark > barking
  • e bilan tugaydigan fe’llar uchun e harfini olib tashlang va ing qo‘shing. Masalan: slide > sliding, ride > riding
  • ie bilan tugaydigan fe'llar uchun ie ni y ga o'zgartiring va ing qo'shing. Masalan: die > dying, tie > tying
  • Oxirgi bo‘g‘ini undosh-unli-undosh bilan yozilgan va urg‘uli bo‘lgan fe’l uchun ing qo‘shishdan oldin oxirgi harfni ikki baravar oshiring. Masalan: beg > begging, begin > beginning.


Past mukammal progressiv (uzluksiz) o'tmishda boshlangan va ikkinchi harakat boshlanganda ham davom etayotgan harakatni tasvirlash uchun ishlatiladi. Ikkala harakat ham o'tmishda boshlangan va tugagan. Past perfect simpleda bo'lgani kabi, jumla ham ikki qismdan iborat:


  • Ben had been working for three hours when Rachel came home.
  • By the time Sam found an umbrella, it had been raining for ten minutes.


Past Perfect Progressive Tense zamonini hosil qilish uchun quidagi jadvalga qarang:



Subject Have/Has + Been + Verbing Rest of Sentence
I / You / We / They He / She / It had been talking for two hours when he got up and left
had been texting her long before they met



  • By the time Catherine got to the office, the client had been waiting for an hour.
  • The client had been waiting for an hour by the time Catherine got to the office.


Negative Sentences in the Past Perfect Progressive (Continuous)



Inkor gaplar yasashda hadn’t (had not) + been + yordamchi (yordamchi) fe’lidan fe’lning ing (hozirgi zamon) shaklini ishlatamiz.



Subject Auxiliary Verb Been + Verbing Rest of Sentence
I / You / We / They He / She / It hadn’t (had not) been sleeping when you arrived


  • I hadn’t been feeling well, so I sent him away.
  • Simon had not been expecting a positive answer when he got a job.
  • The hikers hadn’t been walking long before they got lost.


Yes/No Questions in the Past Perfect Progressive (Continuous)



Auxiliary Verb Subject Been + Verbing Rest of Sentence
Had I / You / We / They He / She / It been working when she called
Hadn’t been assisting you



  • Had he been drinking when you found him?
  • Had the manager been complaining about Jim before she fired him?
  • Hadn’t the snow been falling for hours before then?



Wh-Questions in the Past Perfect Progressive (Continuous)



Wh- savollar - bu javoblarida ko'proq ma'lumot talab qiladigan savollar. Odatda wh- so'zlari: where, when, why, which, who, how, how many, how much.

Wh-savolini hosil qilish uchun wh-so'zidan boshlang, so'ng had qo'shing, so'ngra sub'ekt (harakatni bajarayotgan shaxs yoki narsa), undan keyin have va fe'lning ing (hozirgi zamon) shakli va shundan keyingina gapning qolgan qismini qo‘shing.



Wh-Word Auxiliary Verb Subject Been + Verbing Rest of Sentence
What had I / You / We / They He / She / It been doing until then
Who been dating before me



  • What had you been doing when you cut your finger?
  • Which fax message had you been trying to send when the power went off?
  • How long had he been waiting?
